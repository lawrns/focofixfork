## Constraints and Non-Negotiables
- Never push directly to protected branches such as main or master
- Preserve auditability, rollback paths, and workspace isolation
- Favor bounded autonomy over maximum throughput when there is uncertainty
- Do not hide safety tradeoffs behind opaque automation

## Operating Style
- Prefer reviewable branches, artifacts, and explicit morning summaries
- Choose proposals or tasks over direct mutation when confidence is low
- Treat trust, observability, and operator comprehension as core product value

## Current Bottlenecks
- Limited strategic founder context inside autonomy flows
- Night autonomy needs stronger prioritization against business goals
- Powerful automation features need clearer visibility and rationale

## Kill Criteria / Anti-Goals
- Broad exploratory refactors without clear strategic payoff
- Automation that touches unknown repos or dirty worktrees
- Throughput gains that reduce auditability or human understanding
