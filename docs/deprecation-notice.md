# Foco Simple Mode - Deprecation Notice

## What's Changing

Foco is evolving to help you focus on what matters most. We're introducing **Simple Mode** - a streamlined experience that removes complexity and lets you get things done faster.

Starting **March 1, 2026**, the following features will be retired:

### Deprecated Features

1. **Gantt Charts** → Replaced by Simple Kanban + Calendar view
2. **Custom Fields** → AI automatically extracts metadata from your tasks
3. **Advanced Table View** → Use the cleaner Simple List view
4. **Time Tracking** → Focus on completion, not hours logged
5. **Goals** → Merged into Milestones for clearer outcomes
6. **Advanced Filters** → AI-powered Smart Inbox surfaces what needs attention
7. **Multiple Kanban Customizations** → One simple, beautiful board
8. **File Uploads** → Seamless integration with Drive/Dropbox

## Why We're Simplifying

After analyzing how thousands of teams use Foco, we discovered something important: **the most productive teams use the simplest features**.

### Benefits of Simple Mode

- **3x Faster Task Creation**: No more filling out custom fields or time estimates
- **60% Less Time in Settings**: One simple board, one clear workflow
- **AI Does the Heavy Lifting**: Automatically categorizes, prioritizes, and surfaces what matters
- **Focus on Outcomes**: Milestones replace goals with clearer success metrics
- **Seamless Integration**: Your files stay in Drive/Dropbox where they belong

## What You Need to Do

### 1. Export Your Data (Before February 28)

We've made it easy to export everything you need:

- **Settings > Export > Full Export** - Downloads all your data as CSV/JSON
- Feature-specific exports available in each deprecated section
- Export remains available through February 28, 2026

### 2. Review the Migration Guide

Our comprehensive [Migration Guide](./migration-guide.md) shows you:
- How each deprecated feature maps to Simple Mode
- Step-by-step export procedures
- New workflows that replace old patterns
- Video tutorials for visual learners

### 3. Prepare Your Team

- Share this notice with your team members
- Schedule a 15-minute review of the migration guide
- Test the new workflows in your existing projects
- Reach out to support with questions (support@foco.app)
